how spam mediates our world

in the early days of the internet, email spam was an eclectic yet pervasive part of the digital world. despite being a nuisance, their euntrepreneurial authenticity spoke to a web that was transparent. now, the world is steeped in the hypermediation of targetted advertisement, digital A/B testing and the commodification of social media. instead of the internet with traces of spam, we now consume spam with traces of the internet.
